# Features

!!! warning "Spoiler warning!"
    Please See [Spoilers](Spoilers.md) before expanding the content of this page!

??? info "TLT Features"
    - A **Rune editor** where you can combine Runes to form valid symbols
    - A **Document editor** where you can mix game language with English
    - **Translation guesses** via an entry box on each Rune
    - **In-line translations** for the runes in your document, based on your guesses
    - **Real-time updates** as you enter or update your translation guesses
    - **Save** and **Open** your documents, to preserve both your translation guesses and document content
